About Us

The Illiterati of Denver is a social book club for awesome people. We meet monthly to discuss our current book, reveal our next book selection, and generally hang out and be awesome.

Currently, meetings are the first Monday of the month and typically take place in-or-around Denver's Highlands neighborhood, either at comfy businesses or at our members' homes. We rotate which member picks the next book, and have read across many genres of fiction and non-fiction.

Joining is easy. Just show up. That's it. If you've read the book, or read a book, even better!

To stay informed as to what we are reading, and when and where our meetings are, subscribe to our newsletter!

June's Selection

H is For Hawk

by Helen Macdonald

Purchase from Amazon Search Denver Public Library

Summary:

The instant New York Times bestseller and award-winning sensation, Helen Macdonald's story of adopting and raising one of nature's most vicious predators has soared into the hearts of millions of readers worldwide. Fierce and feral, her goshawk Mabel's temperament mirrors Helen's own state of grief after her father's death, and together raptor and human "discover the pain and beauty of being alive" (People). H Is for Hawk is a genre-defying debut from one of our most unique and transcendent voices.
